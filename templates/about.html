About This Calculator

🇧🇪 What is Belgian TOB Tax?

The Tax on Stock Exchange Transactions (Taks op de beursverrichtingen / Taxe sur les opérations de bourse) is a Belgian tax levied on the purchase and sale of securities. The standard rate for shares is 0.35% of the transaction amount, with a maximum of €1,600 per transaction.

📊 How This Calculator Works

1. PDF Extraction

The system automatically detects your broker (Interactive Brokers or Saxo Bank) and extracts all stock transactions from your PDF statements. It identifies:

  • Transaction dates
  • Stock symbols
  • Buy/Sell type
  • Number of shares
  • Transaction amounts and currencies

2. Transaction Grouping

Same-side transactions are grouped: Multiple purchases or sales of the same stock on the same day are combined into a single transaction.

Day trades remain separate: If you buy AND sell the same stock on the same day, both transactions are kept separate because Belgian law taxes both sides of a day trade.

3. Currency Conversion

All foreign currency amounts are converted to EUR using official European Central Bank (ECB) reference rates for each transaction date. The calculator fetches the latest rates automatically.

4. TOB Calculation

TOB is calculated as: EUR Amount × 0.35%

The maximum TOB per transaction is €1,600 (for transactions above €457,143).

📥 How to Download Interactive Brokers Statements

  1. Log in to your Interactive Brokers account
  2. Navigate to Performance & Reports → Statements
  3. Select Activity Statement (monthly or custom period)
  4. Choose PDF format and download

Note: Activity Statements work best. Other statements with "Trades" sections may also work.

📥 How to Download Saxo Bank Statements

  1. Log in to your Saxo Bank account
  2. Navigate to Account Statements
  3. Click Download Statements
  4. Select Transaction and Balance Report
  5. Choose your date range and download as PDF

Note: Make sure to select "Transaction and Balance Report" format.

Exchange Rate Source
